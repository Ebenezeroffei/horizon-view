About us

Horizon View Apartments is located in the safe, upmarket, serene and tranquil area of East Airport with easy access to essential activities. Our Apartments are fully furnished providing a comfortable home for both short and long term stay. Available are one, two and three bedroom units. Our lovely staff are always ready to make you feel at home

  • Accra-Spintex highway to Tema.
  • Five minute drive to the motorway (The motorway leading to the East toward Ada & Togo & Nigeria,Northwards toward Akosombo & westwards toward Kasoa, Cape Coast,Takoradi & Cote D'Ivoire).
  • Five minute drive to the under-bridge connecting to East Legon, University of Ghana, Legon toward Aburi gardens, Accra Plains & the Eastern Mountains & Escarpment.
